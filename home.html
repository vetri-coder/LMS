About Our Library

Welcome to The Digital Library. Your Gateway to Infinite Knowledge. At The Digital Library, we believe in the power of knowledge to transform lives. Whether you're a student, researcher, professional, or a curious mind, our digital platform offers a vast collection of resources to fuel your learning journey. Explore, discover, and grow with us. At The Digital Library, we believe that learning is a lifelong adventure. We've created a vibrant online community where individuals can explore, share, and expand their knowledge. Our extensive digital collection is carefully curated to cater to a wide range of interests and academic pursuits. Discover new perspectives, delve into fascinating topics, and connect with fellow learners in our dynamic digital space.

Offline Library Rules and Regulations

  1. Students shall scan their ID card in the E Gate Register scanner while entering and leaving the library.
  2. Books shall be issued only on production of Bar-coded ID cards.
  3. Strict silence shall be maintained in the library.
  4. Strict silence shall be maintained in the library.
  5. Mobile phones and other devices should be in silent mode.
  6. Laptops may be used inside the libraries (without any audio).
  7. For the benefit of its users, library is following the Open Access System. The books, current journals, back volumes taken out the racks shall not be replaced. They should be left only on the tables. Well trained Library assistants are there to replace them in the proper places
  8. Library books should be handled with utmost care. Marking or any kind of scribbling on pages or folding of page is strictly prohibited. Mishandling or damage to the books will invite penalty.
  9. In case of loss of any book, the borrower as to pay the current price of the book and a fine decided by the Principal.
  10. Reference books, Project Reports, Back Volumes and Periodicals will not be issued. They are only for reference inside the library.
